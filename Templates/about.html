An Optimized Convolutional Neural Network Approach for detecting Lung Diseases Based on Chest X-Rays

Chest diseases are common throughout the globe. These include pneumonia, asthma, fibrosis, lung cancer etc: Timely diagnosis of lung diseases is essential for treatment and prevention of further complications that could arise. Many researches have been carried out for the individual diagnosis of a particular chest disease. Only a few researches exist with regard to the identification of multiple chest diseases. According to the domain experts, knowing the patientâ€™s medical history and medical related patient data can significantly impact the differential diagnosis of chest diseases. This research is an attempt to use patient medical data such as patient age and patient gender etc: in addition to the patient chest x-ray image data, in order to develop a high accuracy model, in contrast to the conventional CNN models which only use image data.
